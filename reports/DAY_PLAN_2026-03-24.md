## Net DD Fix Applied Today
Yesterday's Net DD values (925%, 1502%) were **wrong** — old formula measured profit swing, not actual capital risk.
**Corrected formula:** Net DD = (Initial Capital - Lowest Capital) / Initial Capital × 100%
- Only >0% when capital drops **below $10,000**
- Max possible = 100% (total wipeout)
- If capital never goes below $10k → Net DD = 0%
All 3 calculation spots fixed in code. Re-running will produce correct values.

**What to expect after re-run:**
